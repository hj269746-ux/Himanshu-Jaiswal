Who is a Farmer?

A farmer is a person engaged in agriculture, raising living organisms for food or raw materials. They work tirelessly to produce the food that sustains us all.



Rice

Why Farmers Are Important

  • Provide food for the entire population
  • Support the economy through agriculture
  • Maintain rural communities and culture
  • Protect and manage natural resources

Farmers play a crucial role in society because they provide the food and raw materials we rely on daily. Without farmers, it would be difficult to sustain communities and economies.
